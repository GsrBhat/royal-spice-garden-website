The Visionary Chef

Crafting Legacies, One Plate at a Time

At Royal Spice Garden, cuisine is more than taste—it is a sensory journey. Led by Chef de Cuisine **Vikram Malhotra**, our kitchen merges the slow-cooked heritage of royal Indian courts with modern, vibrant global flavors.

"Spices are like musical notes; they must be structured in perfect harmony to create a masterpiece. We roast, grind, and infuse our secret spice blends daily to bring you an unparalleled dining experience."

"Every dish we serve carries a piece of history, reimagined for the modern connoisseur."

Chef Vikram Malhotra Executive Chef & Founder
